About Epic: The Musical

Epic: The Musical is a musical adaptation of Homer's The Odyssey created by Jorge Rivera-Herrans

The creation of the musical was documented through Jorge's TikTok, and the song clips and process videos have gone viral on social media.

Jorge Rivera-Herrans started working on the musical around June of 2019 for his senior thesis for college. He wanted to create a musical inspired by video games and anime to "emulate their addictive sense of progression"

EPIC was originally going to be an adaptation of both The Iliad and The Odyssey, which ended up being a huge undertaking that did not work out. Jorge eventually decided to focus on the Odyssey because it has a wider cast and a variety of locations, although he has stated that he may create a musical based on The Iliad after EPIC has concluded.
